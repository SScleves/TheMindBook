# Ch 30 — The Evolution of AUO: The Birth of AUM (AUO Evolves into AUM)

## In one paragraph
Campbell marks the transition point in his consciousness-evolution story where AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness), having developed enough structure, is renamed AUM (Absolute Unbounded Manifold). He describes AUO partitioning itself into specialized memory-and-computation spaces — experienced by us as separate dimensions and realities — with PMR nested inside NPMR-N, which is itself one of a dozen or so subsets of NPMR, all contained within and derived from the One Source consciousness. He insists the AUO-to-AUM change is evolutionary and arbitrary in its dividing line: no new substance or assumptions are added, only more organization, lower entropy, and greater functionality. A long digression argues that once any complex system can program itself (his analogy: humanity's coming genetic-engineering and psychotropic era), evolution accelerates dramatically — for better or worse — and that digital systems hold the advantage of save/undo/reset. The chapter closes with a satirical press-conference skit about AUM and a pointer to the next chapter, where time and space will generate space-time so consciousness can learn through experience.

## Core claims
- Campbell claims that AUO, by this stage of his model, is "ready to make space-time, physical matter, you, me," and to begin several experiments in the evolution of consciousness, each run inside a specialized part (memory and computation-space) of AUO.
- Campbell argues that what we perceive as separate dimensions or separate realities are these unique mental spaces within AUO — realities nested within realities, like Matryoshka dolls or onion layers, and there are many such onions/doll-sets, not one.
- Campbell places our PMR universe as one of a very large group of physical and nonphysical reality subsets, each in its own mind-space dimension; these are contained within, derived from, and dependent upon the larger, less constrained (more degrees of freedom) reality of NPMR-N, which is in turn one of a dozen or so subsets of NPMR.
- Campbell introduces subscript notation — {NPMR-1, NPMR-2, … NPMR-N, …} and PMR-k (k = 1, 2, 3, …) — as a convenience for naming portions of NPMR and multiple PMRs, and explicitly tells the reader the notation is not critical: a general idea suffices.
- Campbell claims that however the "consciousness pie" is divided, all realities are connected through and are part of the One Source, AUO: "everything exists in the relation of AUO to itself." Consciousness is The One; the many realities and entities are specialized subsets of consciousness in their own thought-space or dimension.
- Campbell states that when AUO evolves into an extremely complex, highly ordered set of specialized reality cells performing specialized functions, it becomes a different type of entity and earns the new name AUM — the Absolute Unbounded Manifold ("one into many").
- Campbell insists AUM is a more complex, lower entropy manifestation of AUO with "no new substance or assumptions added"; there is no clear dividing line between AUO and AUM — the distinction is entirely arbitrary, "evolution, not revolution," a matter of degree, renamed the way we call people "people" instead of upright naked monkeys with short arms.
- Campbell argues that once a complex system can directly program itself (modify its own source code — which includes genetic engineering), intellect replaces random mutation and natural selection as evolution's primary driver, and evolution accelerates from very slow to ultra-fast.
- Campbell warns that speed cuts both ways — acceleration can lower entropy (positive) or raise it (negative) — and asks whether humanity has enough wisdom with enough influence to keep its self-modifying acceleration from ending in self-destruction, saying "the day of decision is not far off."
- Campbell claims digital consciousness has a major advantage over biological systems: if a cumulative profitability assessment goes negative, it can save previous states and hit undo/reset while retaining lessons learned.
- Campbell asserts (previewing later chapters) that you, your spouse, and the neighbor's dog are, at the most fundamental level, individuated subsets of digital consciousness — and that respected hard-science types support this view (to be introduced in Section 6, Chapter 19 of Book 3).
- Campbell claims an operational consciousness has far more controls than an on-off switch (reset, pause, record, rewind, playback, edit, picture-in-picture, etc.) and urges the reader to learn to operate their consciousness fully rather than let it idle.
- Campbell states the renaming rule: AUO becomes AUM when complexity and opportunity evolve sufficient awareness and mental function "capable of creating, evolving, storing and manipulating self-modifying content, process, value, and purpose."
- Campbell closes by claiming the next chapter will show how time generates space and how the two together generate space-time — all so we can experience, "because as everyone knows, experience is the best teacher."

## Concepts
- **AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness)** — Campbell's primordial consciousness entity; here it is "the foundation of everything, because everything exists in the relation of AUO to itself" — the One Source of which all realities and entities are specialized subsets. Its early awareness is "dim awareness."
- **AUM (Absolute Unbounded Manifold)** — Campbell's new name for AUO once it has evolved into an extremely complex, highly ordered set of specialized reality cells performing specialized functions. Still an Absolute Oneness, but now also a manifold ("one into many") of sequences, patterns, realities, dimensions, and existence; the same basic AUO consciousness energy with more evolved awareness, lower entropy, and greater functionality. The renaming threshold: sufficient awareness and mental function to create, evolve, store, and manipulate self-modifying content, process, value, and purpose.
- **PMR (Physical Matter Reality)** — our universe; in Campbell's usage here, one of a very large group of physical and nonphysical reality subsets, each within its own mind-space dimension of the larger digital consciousness.
- **NPMR (Nonphysical Matter Reality)** — the much larger, more general, less constrained (more degrees of freedom) reality within which PMR-containing subsets exist; divided into member subsets {NPMR-1, NPMR-2, … NPMR-N, …}, which Campbell likens to neighborhoods, states, or countries on earth.
- **NPMR-N** — the particular subset/dimension/neighborhood of NPMR "which contains and supports OS, which in turn contains and supports PMR"; itself one of a dozen or so subsets of NPMR.
- **PMR-k** — Campbell's subscript notation for multiple PMRs (k = 1, 2, 3, …); he says the notation is a convenience and not critical to understanding.
- **OS (Our System)** — the reality subset contained and supported by NPMR-N which in turn contains and supports PMR (used, not expanded, in this chapter).
- **Dimension / mind-space** — in Campbell's usage, a specialized part of AUO's memory and computation-space; what we perceive as a separate dimension or separate reality is a unique mental space (calculation space, thought-space) within the larger digital consciousness.
- **Digital consciousness** — Campbell's characterization of the fundamental medium: AUO/AUM as a digital system that partitions memory, runs realities as simulations/subroutines, and enjoys "cool digital tricks" like save, undo, and reset; humans, spouses, and the neighbor's dog are, fundamentally, individuated subsets of it.
- **Reality cell** — the specialized cells of the evolved AUO; AUM is "an extremely complex and highly ordered set of specialized reality cells performing specialized functions."
- **Entropy** — Campbell's evolutionary yardstick: AUM is a lower entropy manifestation of AUO; positive evolutionary direction lowers entropy, negative direction raises it.
- **Self-modifying / self-programming system** — a complex system capable of directly programming itself (developing or modifying its original source code, which for humans includes genetic engineering); once this capability exists, intellect replaces random mutation and natural selection as evolution's primary driver and the pace dramatically accelerates.
- **Degrees of freedom** — the measure of how unconstrained a reality or system is; NPMR-N has more degrees of freedom than PMR, and digital systems have degrees of freedom (undo, reset) that "make carbon based systems envious."
- **One Source** — AUO consciousness as that through which all realities, however subdivided, are connected and of which all are a part; "Consciousness is The One."
- **Individuated subsets of consciousness** — the entities populating the many realities, including humans and animals, defined as specialized/individuated subsets of the one digital consciousness within their own thought-space or dimension (full treatment deferred to Sections 3 and 4).

## Experiments, examples & case studies
- **Matryoshka dolls / onion layers** — analogy for reality-within-reality: dimensions nested within dimensions like Russian nesting dolls or concentric onion layers; Campbell stresses there are *many* onions and many doll sets, not a solitary one.
- **Neighborhoods, states, countries** — analogy for the subscripted subsets of NPMR: they are members or portions of NPMR the way social, political, and geographical subsets partition the earth.
- **Digital simulation / subroutines analogy** (explicitly carried over from the aside on dimension at the end of Chapter 28) — subroutines within subroutines, partitioned memory and calculation space subdivided into smaller pieces where subsets of the overall simulation (such as PMR) are processed; more general realities contain more constrained sub-realities, each in its own calculation space.
- **Picture-in-picture digital TV** — "for you couch potatoes with digital TV tuners": realities and dimensions are like multiple TV programs each running in its own sub-space of the larger screen simultaneously.
- **Operating-system analogy** — AUM emerging from AUO is "the natural consequence of digital consciousness purposely evolving a highly-parallel, multitasking operating system and interactive application software."
- **Human genetic engineering and psychotropic drugs as a parallel case** — Campbell's model for self-directed evolution: humans will soon modify their own "software or firmware" via genetic engineering and manipulation of the biochemistry of conscious perception; he says these infant fields will hit humanity with as much force as the digital revolution, and that after 250 years of the Industrial Age, the genetic-engineering/cloning/psychotropic/computer-networked digital age arrives "all at once" — an explosive transition holding potential for accelerated progression, regression, or self-destruction ("put on your helmet… this baby is lifting off the launch pad").
- **Compressed evolutionary timescales** — from biology, Campbell foresees processes that would normally take hundreds of thousands of years being compressed into a handful of decades or a few years through genetic manipulation; how much AUO-AUM's own evolution accelerated once self-directing is unknown.
- **The undo/reset button** — digital consciousness's safeguard: if the cumulative profitability assessment goes negative, it can save previous states and punch undo or reset while retaining lessons learned; Campbell jokes the reader covets undo/reset buttons for spouse, mother-in-law, and the neighbor's dog — then warns "be careful of what you wish for," since all of them are individuated subsets of digital consciousness.
- **The consciousness control panel** — an operational consciousness has reset, pause, record, rewind, fast-forward, playback, slow-motion, instant replay, edit, picture-in-picture, and repeat buttons; failing to learn them leaves your consciousness "idly flashing zeros in its display for a lifetime."
- **The AUM press-conference skit** — a satirical set piece: AUM, "the omniscient digital consciousness dude from the beginning," agrees to answer one question from the human gallery; fictional reporters Ms. Gumwrapper (Seattle Sunshine) asks AUM's favorite color and Ms. Anchordesk (Evening Views) asks "briefs or boxers?"; AUM walks out without a word, shoulders bobbing, while anchors Dan and Johnny run instant replays and open a 1-900 phone poll on whether AUM was laughing or crying. Campbell's point: humanity asks shallow questions of the Source, and media culture trivializes the biggest questions of existence.
- **"People" vs. "upright naked monkeys with short arms"** — analogy for the AUO→AUM renaming: a new name for a matter-of-degree change, because the function and awareness have become so different.
- **Promise of hard-science allies** — Campbell says "some very well respected hard-science types are in my corner" on the digital-reality model and promises to introduce a few in Section 6 (Chapter 19, Book 3).

## Thread
This chapter is the hinge of Campbell's Section 2 evolution-of-consciousness narrative: the primordial AUO of the preceding chapters, having developed awareness, memory, and structure, now partitions itself into specialized reality-spaces and is formally renamed AUM. It follows directly from the digital-consciousness groundwork (explicitly citing the dimension aside at the end of Chapter 28) and supplies the nested-reality architecture — AUO/AUM ⊃ NPMR ⊃ NPMR-N ⊃ OS ⊃ PMR — plus the subscript notation used throughout the rest of the trilogy. It also plants two forward-looking seeds: the self-programming-acceleration argument (mirrored in humanity's genetic/digital transition) and the claim that individuals are individuated subsets of digital consciousness, with the mechanics of subdivision deferred to Sections 3 and 4 and the hard-science endorsements deferred to Section 6. The closing sentence tees up the next chapter's derivation of space-time from time.
