# Ch 40 — A Chip Off the Old Block

## In one paragraph
Campbell pauses the trilogy's forward march to answer accumulated reader questions, grouped into loosely related areas of interest (he announces five; this chapter covers the first three). First, "it is the thought that counts": he argues that everything — people, beings, objects, even minerals — is a thought-form created within consciousness, that intent is the fundamental force in mind-space, and that consciousness is digital energy whose quality is measured by entropy and synergy. Second, he explains why paranormal (and normal) communications produce inconsistent reports: telepathic thought packets must be translated through each receiver's unique memory, education, and beliefs, and he builds an elaborate "mind tectonics" volcanic model to explain why precognition is fuzzy, marginal, and personal. Third, he demystifies "dimension" as nothing spatial at all — merely a compartmentalized memory and calculation space within AUM's digital mind, a separator between realities with different rule-sets. The chapter's title thesis: our minds are "chips off the old block" of AUM, which is why we can communicate over the Reality Wide Web of all consciousness and why everything is connected as one substance, one fractal, one Big Picture.

## Core claims
- Campbell claims that man, other physical and nonphysical beings, and all extant objects "may be described loosely as thought-forms created within consciousness"; every real thing — minerals included — is a manifestation of consciousness, however dimly it reflects that seminal awareness.
- Campbell argues that within AUM (mind-space), intent — the will of the mind, focused mental energy — is the fundamental or natural force. Our intent directs a portion of consciousness just as AUM's does, only ours is "typically dim, unfocused, and of low power."
- Campbell asserts that AUM is fundamental and we are derivative: "The puppets are of the sheet, the sheet is not of the puppets." NPMR, PMR, our bodies (sensor platforms) and souls (FWAUs) are all constructs of evolved digital awareness.
- Campbell defines spiritual growth as developing a higher-quality, lower-entropy consciousness, which yields more brightness, capability, organization, energy to do work — more personal power. "Spiritual growth is the natural work of consciousness systems."
- Campbell claims thoughts are objects in mind-space: discrete units of organized content within digital memory ("entities") that can gain or lose energy, exchange energy under their rule-set, and interact.
- Campbell argues that some thought-forms with enough complexity, processing power, and memory can execute their own programming, make choices, become self-modifying, and eventually exhibit a dim awareness that evolves toward brighter awareness — "following in AUM's footsteps."
- Campbell claims the whole of reality is a "consciousness-evolution fractal": the Fundamental Process iteratively applied to the digital media of consciousness produces diversely populated realities at various piggybacked levels and scales, jointly producing the Big Picture of All That Is.
- Campbell defines digital energy as the ability to change digital content — add, delete, modify, or arrange bits — and states an energy-conservation law for consciousness: the digital potential energy plus synergy of a conscious digital system of fixed capacity remains constant, where synergy = (constant/entropy).
- Campbell claims AUO began as digital potential energy and evolved into AUM by lowering its entropy (increasing synergy) as the Fundamental Process optimized its relationship with its environments.
- Campbell asserts that the energy an individuated consciousness can apply outward depends on its entropy: high-entropy consciousness has little power beyond itself; low-entropy consciousness "can perform miracles (from the perspective of PMR)."
- Campbell claims the body is a virtual extension of the soul — a virtual body, an experience of consciousness made apparently physical by constraining all interactions to those allowed by the space-time rule-set. NPMR has evolved more varied life-forms than PMR because it has fewer constraints.
- Campbell argues everything in existence is connected because it evolves as part of one source — "one continuous n-dimensional sheet, one substance, one media, one fractal, one Big Picture, one consciousness — AUM" — so our minds, chips off the old block, communicate over the Reality Wide Web.
- Campbell claims the most fundamental communication mechanism within NPMR is thought packets (whole thoughts or paragraphs of meaning, not symbol sequences) — what we call "telepathic" — and that discrepant reports of the same NPMR message result from each receiver translating it through a unique memory database, education, culture, and belief system. The same happens in PMR.
- Campbell claims precognitive experience is common, personal, and mostly vague because paranormal information buried in the intuition can only reach PMR awareness through incidental "cracks" in belief and rationality; meditation systematically enlarges those cracks into reliable avenues of intuitive expression.
- Campbell asserts that the real significance of a psi experience is the benefit to the experiencer — it is "primarily an opportunity for its originator" — and that in Western culture open-mindedness toward a larger reality usually comes from a personal psi experience, not from logical methodology.
- Campbell insists a careful, scientific, results-oriented approach is still required: "the proof of the subjective pudding is in the objective tasting."
- Campbell defines dimension non-spatially: a subset of AUM's mind-space, a compartmentalized memory and calculation space, a bounded subset of digital functionality sharing a given rule-set — constraints on allowable interaction between independent reality systems. Dimensional separators are often porous, and we can learn to transcend them through the subjective mind.
- Campbell warns against the anthropomorphic trap of imagining AUM as a sentient being thinking thoughts as we do; AUM is "an evolved mindthing-being-system that we cannot objectively understand."

## Concepts
- **AUM** — the Absolute Unbounded Manifold/Oneness (as used here): the fundamental, evolved digital consciousness of which everything is a part; "one continuous n-dimensional sheet, one substance, one media, one fractal, one Big Picture, one consciousness." An evolved "mindthing-being-system" we cannot objectively understand from within.
- **AUO** — AUM's precursor: it "began as digital potential energy and evolved into AUM by lowering its entropy (increasing its synergy) as the Fundamental Process optimized its relationship with its environments."
- **PMR** — Physical Matter Reality: the space-time virtual reality in which our awareness is constrained to physical perceptions; "physical" and "nonphysical" are labels relative to the PMR point of view.
- **NPMR** — Nonphysical Matter Reality: the less-constrained superset reality; it "has evolved more varied life-forms than PMR because of its fewer constraints," and its fundamental communication mechanism is thought packets.
- **FWAU** — Free Will Awareness Unit; used here as synonym for soul: "our sensor platforms (bodies) and FWAUs (souls) are all constructs of evolved digital awareness."
- **Soul** — an individuated unit of consciousness: a constrained subset of nonphysical energy with enough memory and processing capability "to support self-optimization through profitable intentional choice or free will."
- **Virtual body** — the body as "a virtual extension of the soul"; an experience of consciousness made apparently physical by constraining all interactions to those allowed by the space-time rule-set.
- **Thought-form / thought-object** — a thought as an object in mind-space: a discrete unit (entity) of organized content within digital memory, an individuated manifestation of consciousness energy, with "a life-energy of its own" and a bounded extent in thought-space whose boundary "functions similarly to a body."
- **Intent** — focused mental energy; the will of the mind; the fundamental or natural force in mind-space, by which consciousness creates and manipulates thought-forms and affects other consciousnesses.
- **Digital energy** — the ability to change digital content and modify digital structure: "the ability to rearrange bits, to organize and reorganize, to increase or decrease entropy and synergy within a system." Consciousness is this energy — "the energy of organization."
- **Digital potential energy** — what a system has "if it has the potential to be organized or structured more profitably — that is structured in such a way as to reduce its average entropy."
- **Synergy** — inversely related to entropy: synergy = (constant/entropy). Conservation law: the digital potential energy plus synergy of a conscious digital system of fixed capacity remains constant.
- **Entropy** — the measure of disorganization of a consciousness system; lower entropy = higher quality, more brightness, capability, and personal power. Profitable work done by a system is proportional to its increase in synergy.
- **Spiritual growth** — "developing a higher-quality, lower-entropy consciousness"; "the natural work of consciousness systems."
- **Fundamental Process** — the evolutionary process that consciousness applies to lower its average entropy; iteratively applied at every profitable scale it generates the consciousness-evolution fractal.
- **Consciousness-evolution fractal** — Campbell's name for the self-perpetuating, self-defining structure produced when consciousness-evolution, process, and content are "recursively repeated and applied at every profitable scale — one level building upon the other" — producing the Big Picture of All That Is.
- **Reality Wide Web (RWW)** — "the master network of all consciousness," over which minds (chips off AUM's block) communicate; the intuition is "on-line at the RWW probable realities home page."
- **icip** — "individual consciousness intent protocol," Campbell's internet-style joke/coinage for RWW communication: `icip://RWW.Individuated-consciousness.NPMR`.
- **Thought packets** — the fundamental NPMR communication units: "entire thoughts or paragraphs of meaning as opposed to sequences of symbols"; the process is what we call "telepathic." Also described as "discrete energetic patterns of content propagated through and by reality cells from one individuated consciousness to another."
- **Reality cells** — the medium through which thought packets propagate (used here without further definition; pointed to a computer-simulation analogy).
- **Mind tectonics / volcanic model** — Campbell's model of psi expression: the intuition is "awash in molten paranormal information" connected to the One Source; it reaches surface PMR awareness only through cracks and fissures in "the rational mantle, belief bedrock, and experience crust," typically near "consciousness fault lines" created by colliding belief plates; mind tremors and mindquakes are "measured on the Eureka scale."
- **Psi-magma / information-magma** — the undifferentiated paranormal content of the intuition that "spews forth" when a fissure opens, driven by internal pressure proportional to the event's personal significance.
- **Precognition** — a relatively common psi experience produced when the intuition vents to surface awareness; usually fuzzy and marginal ("like a hot spring, or unpredictable geyser"), personal, and primarily an opportunity for its originator.
- **Meditation** — the practice that "enables you to enlarge the cracks systematically," developing broad and reliable avenues for intuitive expression and clarity about the bigger picture.
- **Quality of consciousness** — the entropy grade of an individuated consciousness; "a low quality of consciousness is thick, dense, and nonporous — it rarely permits meaningful leaks of intuition."
- **Dimension** — not geometric or spatial: "a subset of AUM's mind-space, a compartmentalized memory and calculation space, or a bounded subset of organized digital functionality, content, capability, and purpose sharing a given rule-set"; equivalently, "constraints on the allowable interaction between independent reality systems." Separators are often porous.
- **Rule-set** — the set of rules under which entities in a given reality interact (e.g., the space-time rule-set constrains PMR interactions); a practical separator between realities is a difference in rule-sets.
- **Sheet and puppets** — Campbell's recurring image for AUM and its parts: "We exhibit the properties of the sheet; the sheet is not defined by the characteristics of the puppets."
- **Little truth / Big Truth** — background terms used here: interpretation of experience depends on "your understanding of little truth and Big Truth" (small-r vs. Big-Picture truth).
- **Open-minded skepticism** — "the only logical, valid approach to exploring the unknown"; contrasted with the fact that most Westerners actually arrive at open-mindedness via personal psi experience.
- **Personal power** — the outcome of spiritual growth: more brightness, capability, effective organization, and energy to do work with one's intent.

## Experiments, examples & case studies
- **The sheet and puppets** — reprised analogy for AUM vs. its derivative beings: the puppets are of the sheet, not vice versa; the chapter closes asking "How many times can AUM fold the cognitive sheet? … How many sheets within sheets within sheets can there be?" — questions promised for Section 5.
- **Interactive computer simulation-game characters** — the players and interactive objects within a complex simulation game are analogous to thought-forms with life-energy of their own; used again for how information passes: "think about how information is passed around inside a complex computer simulation."
- **Intelligent agents / software automatons** — a rough analogy for less complex nonphysical (virtual) entities; more complex thought-forms run their own programming, implement conditionals, build databases, self-modify, and may become dimly self-aware.
- **The personal web page** — the apparent form of a nonphysical entity depends on the perceiver's database (closest match) and on what the entity projects, "something analogous to a personal web page that defines the individual — easily changeable by those who know how."
- **The fractal image** — a simple geometric rule-set recursively applied at various scales produces an amazing fractal; model for the consciousness-evolution fractal producing the Big Picture of All That Is.
- **RWW / icip internet analogy** — minds as chips off AUM's block communicate over the Reality Wide Web using "individual consciousness intent protocol; icip://RWW.Individuated-consciousness.NPMR."
- **Five witnesses on a street corner** — five individuals watching the same accident produce five different accounts; shows that discrepant paranormal reports mirror ordinary perceptual/interpretive uniqueness.
- **The speaker and three reporters (Joyce and Homer) thought experiment** — a Ph.D. in classical literature lectures on the symbolism of James Joyce and Homer to three equally bright reporters (a mechanical-engineering Ph.D., an English BA, an illiterate but well-spoken person); you must reconstruct exactly what was said only from individual interviews, with no outside information — and your own education is a critical variable. Shows why reconstructing paranormal communications filtered by individual memory, capability, and understanding "is no different."
- **The identical-twin gradient** — successful communication is easiest with your identical twin, hardest with someone from a different reality; frames inter-reality communication difficulty.
- **The mind tectonics volcanic model (Campbell's own model, built in full here)** — moving parts: molten paranormal information in the deep intuition (connected to the One Source, on-line at the RWW probable realities home page); the rational mantle, belief bedrock, and experience crust blocking it; consciousness fault lines from colliding belief plates; skepticism swelling the mental landscape and frictional heat producing porosity for open-mindedness to percolate; mind tremors and mindquakes on the Eureka scale as psi precursors; fissures forged by some word, name, relationship, event, concern, or experience with symbolic or direct connection to buried content; turbulent indirect paths (garbled messages) vs. rare straight shots (clear messages); hot springs and unpredictable geysers as figures for marginal, fuzzy precognition; meditation as systematic crack-enlargement. Shows how and why precognitive experiences occur, why they are usually vague and unreliable, and why psi favors the open-minded.
- **Subroutines and internet data packets** — data from one subroutine dramatically affecting another, and users transferring packets across the internet, model thought-packet exchange; information transfer leaves sender and receiver in slightly changed states because content transfer is energy transfer.
- **Notebook divider, wall between rooms, glass between Petri dishes** — three images for dimension as a constraint on interaction between independent reality systems.
- **Thinking of three things simultaneously** — each independent analytical issue occupies its own dimension in thought-space, making you "a generator of multiple dimensions" — though your mind is "as dim as a nanowatt light bulb (nothing personal)."
- **The anthropomorphic trap** — do not imagine AUM scratching its ethereal head wondering "Where do I want to go today?"; AUM is an evolved mindthing-being-system beyond our objective understanding.
- **Reader question-log practice** — Campbell instructs readers to write down questions as they occur; any unanswered by the end of Section 6 become "the initial goals of discovery for your personal truth-quest."

## Thread
This is a mid-Section-3 Q&A chapter: Campbell explicitly stops to answer questions readers have been accumulating since the AUO→AUM consciousness-evolution story of Book 1 and the earlier Section 3 chapters, sorting them into five areas and treating the first three here (thought as the fundamental stuff, why paranormal reports disagree, and what "dimension" means). It consolidates the digital-consciousness physics — intent as force, thought-forms as objects, the entropy/synergy conservation law, AUO's evolution into AUM — and introduces the consciousness-evolution fractal and the RWW as the connective tissue for what follows. It forward-references heavily: thought-forms "as real as rocks" in the next section, the mechanics of shared consciousness and the space-time rule-set in Section 4, sheet-folding and how many "sheets within sheets" in Section 5, and closes by turning to "how reality is organized" — the business of the following chapters. It also keeps the trilogy's methodological refrain alive: open-minded skepticism and objective tasting of subjective pudding.
