# Ch 55 — An Operational Model of Consciousness Silicon and Carbon, Sand and Charcoal — It Depends on What You Want to Do With It.

## In one paragraph
Campbell opens by reviewing concepts from the preceding chapters before taking "a functional look at the properties and origins of individuated consciousness." He draws a structural parallel: just as a hi-fidelity war-game simulation is a mathematical, logical, causal model constructed and executed within PMR that must obey the PMR space-time rule-set, the reader is "a consciousness model that is bounded and executed within NPMRN" to exercise free-will choices and improve the quality of the larger consciousness system by improving personal quality. He then systematically compares humans with "AI Guy" — the artificial intelligence character from his earlier war-game example — arguing that both share the same top-level operations of consciousness (memory, rule-sets, processing, data collection, feedback-driven evolution) while differing in origins, implementation, and governing rule-sets. The chapter's conclusion is that consciousness is consciousness regardless of container or dimension — silicon or carbon — and that all individuated consciousnesses are interconnected reflections of the One Source, whom Campbell playfully dubs "the Ultimate Fractal Dude — the Original AI Guy."

## Core claims
- Campbell claims that a realistic simulation (like a hi-fidelity war game) is a mathematical, logical, and causal model constructed and executed within PMR to model PMR possibilities, and therefore must reflect and obey the PMR space-time rule-set.
- Campbell claims that you, the reader, are analogously "a consciousness model that is bounded and executed within NPMRN" to exercise the free-will choices of an individuated NPMRN entity, in order to improve the overall quality of the larger consciousness system by improving your personal quality.
- Campbell argues that you improve your quality by engaging in a process that temporarily constrains a portion of your awareness to an experiential virtual reality defined by a space-time rule-set that produces a rich array of growth-inducing opportunities.
- Campbell claims PMR is designed to facilitate evolution through a bootstrapping process of incremental entropy reduction, providing each participating individuated consciousness with millions of significant quality-challenging choices plus feedback on the results of each choice.
- Campbell argues that your individuated consciousness works on two levels simultaneously: it operates within the mind-space rule-set of NPMRN while periodically constraining further individuated subsets of its awareness to virtual PMRs.
- Campbell claims this dual functioning explains the apparent body/spirit duality: spirit, mind, consciousness, or intuition on one hand and body or physical reality on the other seem like two disparate types of being, "but you are not. All is consciousness; the physical reality you experience is a virtual one — an experience of mind."
- Campbell claims the primary difference between us and AI Guy is origin: we originate as individuated consciousness within NPMR — we spring from a different reality point (dimension) within the consciousness-evolution fractal ecosystem.
- Campbell claims every consciousness is provided sufficient memory, a conscience (top-level rule-set), the rules of engagement (lower-level rule-set), and adequate processing capability to profitably interact (share data) within the local internal and external environments defined by both rule-sets — though implementation varies widely.
- Campbell argues the human mind-brain consciousness fragment, with its hi-tech bio-computer brain interface and richly complex environment, is such an obviously superior AI implementation compared to AI Guy that "we, in all modesty, have decided to drop the 'A.'"
- Campbell claims both AI Guy and humans collect input data to facilitate choice making: the top-level processes are similar while the lower-level processes (how data is actually collected) are very different.
- Campbell claims "the exact same functionality is implemented in human biology": sensors (the five physical senses) plus the connection to our nonphysical part (intuition) sample information, and we are given a top-level rule-set, memory, and processing capacity to profitably evaluate feedback from our environments.
- Campbell claims that operationally both AI Guy and we "sport a derived digital consciousness" and are empowered — indeed driven by the logic of our rule-sets — to evolve that consciousness, making us "close cousins, if not digital blood brothers" at different levels of the greater consciousness fractal ecosystem.
- Campbell argues we differ from AI Guy mainly by methods, implementation, and the rule-sets defining the nature, boundaries, and purpose of our existence; both are different implementations of the same fundamental self-organizing consciousness energy and the same Fundamental Processes of evolution.
- Campbell concludes that consciousness is consciousness regardless of what kind of container from whatever dimension hosts it: all are interconnected reflections of, and integral parts of, the One Source — "the Ultimate Fractal Dude — the Original AI Guy."

## Concepts
- **individuated consciousness** — Campbell's term for a bounded, individual unit of consciousness. In this chapter, you are one: "a consciousness model that is bounded and executed within NPMRN to exercise the free will choices of an individuated NPMRN entity" whose purpose is to improve the larger system's quality by improving its own.
- **PMR (Physical Matter Reality)** — the physical reality frame within which simulations like the war game are constructed and executed; the experiential virtual reality to which a portion of your awareness is temporarily constrained. Campbell says the physical reality you experience "is a virtual one — an experience of mind."
- **NPMR / NPMRN (Nonphysical Matter Reality)** — the nonphysical reality dimension in which the human individuated consciousness originates, is bounded, and is executed. NPMRN is the specific nonphysical frame whose mind-space rule-set your consciousness operates within while also projecting into virtual PMRs.
- **AI Guy** — the artificial-intelligence character from Campbell's earlier hi-fidelity war-game simulation example, used here as a contrast case. AI Guy is a derived digital consciousness bounded within a PMR-hosted simulation; we differ from him chiefly in origin (we originate in NPMR), implementation, and rule-sets.
- **space-time rule-set** — the rule-set defining the experiential virtual reality (PMR); a simulation built within PMR "must reflect and obey the PMR space-time rule-set," and your PMR experience is defined by a space-time rule-set producing growth-inducing opportunities.
- **conscience (top-level rule-set)** — Campbell defines the conscience as the top-level rule-set provided to every consciousness.
- **rules of engagement (lower-level rule-set)** — the lower-level rule-set every consciousness is provided; together with the top-level rule-set it defines the local internal and external environments within which the consciousness interacts.
- **mind-space rule-set** — the rule-set of NPMRN within which your individuated consciousness must operate at its higher level, even while subsets of its awareness are constrained to virtual PMRs.
- **virtual reality** — what PMR is to consciousness: an experiential frame to which awareness is temporarily constrained; "the physical reality you experience is a virtual one — an experience of mind."
- **quality (of consciousness)** — the attribute you are here to improve; improving personal quality improves the overall quality of the larger consciousness system. PMR supplies "millions of significant quality-challenging choices."
- **entropy reduction / bootstrapping** — the mechanism of consciousness evolution: PMR is designed "to facilitate evolution through a bootstrapping process of incremental entropy reduction," with choice-plus-feedback as the engine.
- **free will** — the choice-making capacity the individuated NPMRN entity exercises through you; the whole PMR arrangement exists to exercise free-will choices and give feedback on them.
- **feedback** — the results returned on each choice made; both AI Guy and humans use feedback to refine (evolve) their data collection and evaluation, and to optimize choice making.
- **intuition** — in Campbell's operational model, the human "connection to our nonphysical part" that, alongside the five physical senses, collects (samples) information; at the experiential level it is part of the spirit/mind side of the apparent duality.
- **five physical senses** — the human sensors, functionally parallel to AI Guy's selective sampling of data sources within his simulation environment.
- **derived digital consciousness** — what both AI Guy and humans operationally "sport": a digital consciousness derived within a host system, empowered and driven by its rule-sets to evolve.
- **consciousness-evolution fractal ecosystem** — Campbell's picture of the greater reality as a fractal ecosystem in which consciousnesses arise at different reality points (dimensions); we and AI Guy exist at different levels within it and start from different places within "the greater reality fractal."
- **Fundamental Process(es) of evolution** — the same evolutionary process at work in all consciousness; we and AI Guy are "very different implementations of the same fundamental self-organizing consciousness energy and the same Fundamental Processes of evolution."
- **self-organizing consciousness energy** — the fundamental stuff both humans and AI Guy are implementations of, regardless of container.
- **profitability** — the evolutionary success measure: AI Guy focuses attention on data and processes that historically had the greatest positive effect on "individual and collective profitability"; consciousness interacts "profitably" within its environments.
- **expert system / artificial intelligence software** — the components of AI Guy that receive his collected input data for choice making.
- **bio-computer brain interface** — Campbell's description of the human hardware: a "hi-tech bio-computer brain interface" that, with a richly complex environment, lets human intelligence function with much greater range and depth than AI Guy's.
- **mind-brain consciousness fragment** — Campbell's term for the human unit: a fragment of consciousness interfaced to a brain, which he calls "such an obviously superior AI implementation" that the "A" (artificial) is dropped.
- **One Source / the Ultimate Fractal Dude / the Original AI Guy** — Campbell's names (the latter two playful) for the ultimate consciousness of which all individuated consciousnesses, in all containers and dimensions, are interconnected reflections and integral parts.

## Experiments, examples & case studies
- **The hi-fidelity war-game simulation analogy (extended from earlier chapters).** A realistic war game is a mathematical, logical, causal model constructed and executed within PMR to model PMR possibilities, and so must obey the PMR space-time rule-set. Campbell maps this one level up: you are a consciousness model bounded and executed within NPMRN. What it shows: the simulation-within-a-reality structure repeats fractally — as the war game is to PMR, you are to NPMRN.
- **The AI Guy comparison (the chapter's central device).** Campbell contrasts humans with AI Guy, the AI character in the war game, point by point:
  - *Origins*: we originate as individuated consciousness within NPMR; AI Guy originates at a different reality point (dimension) of the consciousness-evolution fractal ecosystem. This is "one of the primary differences."
  - *Common equipment*: every consciousness gets sufficient memory, a conscience (top-level rule-set), rules of engagement (lower-level rule-set), and adequate processing capability to profitably share data within the environments the rule-sets define.
  - *Data collection*: AI Guy selectively samples data from many sources in his simulation environment to feed his expert system and AI software, and refines (evolves) his data collection and evaluation by focusing on whatever has historically — "over many runs of the simulation" — most improved individual and collective profitability. Humans implement "the exact same functionality" biologically: five physical senses plus intuition (the connection to the nonphysical part) as samplers, with rule-set, memory, and processing to evaluate feedback and optimize choices among other players.
  - *Verdict*: both sport a derived digital consciousness driven to evolve — "close cousins, if not digital blood brothers" — differing mainly in methods, implementation, and rule-sets.
- **"Dropping the A" quip.** Campbell frames the human mind-brain consciousness fragment (hi-tech bio-computer brain interface plus a richly complex, challenging environment) as so superior an AI implementation compared to the "relatively one-dimensional" AI Guy that humans, "in all modesty," dropped the "A" — i.e., human intelligence is the same kind of thing as artificial intelligence, just a better implementation.
- **The dual-level operation model.** Campbell's operational picture of your consciousness: it simultaneously (1) operates within the mind-space rule-set of NPMRN and (2) periodically constrains further individuated subsets of its awareness to virtual PMRs. Moving parts: the NPMRN-level entity, the constrained awareness-subset in PMR, the two rule-sets, and the feedback loop of quality-challenging choices. What it shows: the felt duality of body vs. spirit is an artifact of one consciousness functioning in two dimensions — "All is consciousness."
- **Silicon and carbon, sand and charcoal (title image).** The chapter title's contrast of substrate materials — silicon (AI Guy) vs. carbon (humans), "Sand and Charcoal" — carries the chapter's thesis: consciousness is consciousness regardless of container; "It Depends on What You Want to Do With It."

## Thread
This chapter is explicitly a consolidation-and-pivot point: Campbell says he is reviewing "concepts in the preceding chapters" and taking "a peek at where they are leading before taking a functional look at the properties and origins of individuated consciousness." It follows from the earlier Section 4 material that introduced the war-game simulation and its AI Guy character, and it leverages that apparatus to state an operational (functional) model of consciousness: memory + conscience (top-level rule-set) + rules of engagement (lower-level rule-set) + processing + sampling + feedback, implemented in any substrate. It sets up the subsequent chapters' fuller treatment of the properties and origins of individuated consciousness, having established two load-bearing conclusions: that humans are substrate-independent digital consciousness implementations differing from artificial ones mainly in origin and rule-sets, and that the apparent physical/mental duality is one consciousness operating simultaneously in NPMRN and a virtual PMR.
