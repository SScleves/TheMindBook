# Ch 65 — Deriving PMR Physics from Consciousness

## In one paragraph
Campbell argues that PMR physicists are stuck looking for a "little TOE" (a PMR-only Theory Of Everything) because, lacking the Big Picture, they hit an "invisible wall constructed of belief" at the edge of their little box and patch the resulting inconsistencies with uncertainty principles instead of breaking through. He recalls the Chapter 31 (Book 1) analogy — reality cells as transistors, AUM's basic cognitive functioning as the processor's instruction-set, the space-time rule-set as assembly-language algorithms, and our experience as an AUM-TBC-to-individuated-consciousness interface analogous to an object-oriented C++ simulation in which we are the objects, with AUM as computer, programmer, and operating system — and adds a new analogy: space-time as a custom Digital Signal Processor (DSP) chip, where understanding the rules governing energy transfer between transistors (reality cells) should yield the fundamental relationships of physics. He proposes that the space-time instruction-set, like Newton's laws or Maxwell's four equations, is a small set of general, high-level statements from which all the detailed implications (the science we know) flow, and that this instruction-set may be discoverable in the geometry of the space-time construct itself — a project Einstein attempted from the PMR side alone (spending the last half of his career on an unsuccessful unified field theory) and correctly intuited but couldn't complete, being too limited by his era's cultural beliefs. Campbell then credits Steven E. Kaufman, a "contemporary resident of PMR," with having actually done this: starting from simple binary reality cells within what Campbell identifies as an AUO (though Kaufman doesn't use that term), Kaufman derives PMR electromagnetism, gravity, force, and energy, and — by reverse engineering — much of classical physics, relativity, and quantum mechanics, all from the properties of reality cells considered only in relation to themselves. Campbell frames Kaufman's book, Unified Reality Theory, as proof of concept that current PMR physics can be shown to be a logical conclusion fully contained within My Big TOE — satisfying the requirement that a Big TOE must fully contain its little TOE and must not conflict with known little-TOE facts while explaining what little TOE cannot (psi effects, mind, consciousness, human purpose, the efficacy of intuition) — and notes that Kaufman's AUO starting assumption is a good sign, since a successful Big TOE must have at least one mystical or metaphysical leg to stand on.

## Core claims
- Campbell claims PMR physicists have been working on a "little PMR TOE" for a long time but are stymied because they do not understand the Big Picture or the larger reality of which their sought TOE is only a part; being unaware a Big TOE exists limits them to searching only for a little TOE.
- He claims that because of the digital algorithmic properties of the PMR rule-set, there may or may not even be a little TOE expressible exclusively in little-TOE logic (PMR mathematics).
- He claims scientists and philosophers, digging out rules one at a time from inside their local reality, hit "an invisible wall constructed of belief" where their little picture meets the larger reality, and that only a conceptual breakthrough that fundamentally expands their reality can break through it.
- He claims "You cannot get out of the box while defining the box to be everything that exists" — if your box is believed to be All That Is, everything outside it looks delusional, so the little TOEs describing the box look like big TOEs to the little-box-scientists trapped inside, and uncertainty principles get brought in to patch the inconsistencies that appear whenever their little TOE is stubbed against the box's limitations.
- He claims you must first understand and appreciate the potential existence of a Big TOE before the little-TOE-to-Big-TOE interface can come into focus, otherwise belief systems limit your reality to a subset of the solution-space that does not contain the answer (with a cross-reference that scientific, cultural, religious, and personal belief systems are discussed in Chapters 19 through 22 of Book 1).
- He recalls the Chapter 31 (Book 1) computer analogy: reality cells are roughly analogous to the transistors on a processor chip (each a thing that can be on or off, a 1 or 0, distorted or undistorted); the processor's basic instruction-set (storing, retrieving, arithmetic/logical operations) is analogous to basic cognitive functioning within AUM; the space-time rule-set is analogous to algorithms written in assembly language; and our experience is generated at the next level of abstraction by an AUM-TBC-to-individuated-consciousness interface analogous to a simulation programmed in object-oriented C++ where we are the objects — with AUM as the computer, the programmer, and the operating system, and sentient beings as bounded, evolving, interactive subsets of reality cells.
- He introduces a Digital Signal Processor (DSP) chip as a further analog to space-time: understanding the rules (patterns) governing energy transfer to and from transistors in a special-purpose microprocessor would reveal the most basic relationships of its design, implementation, and capacity; likewise, understanding the rules governing information transfer between space-time reality cells should produce some of physics's most fundamental relationships — with a forward pointer that "In Section 4, you will see how that works."
- He claims physical experience is generated when an individuated consciousness's perception is constrained to follow the space-time rule-set, likened to a specialized space-time virtual reality trainer operating within a subset of digital calculation-space called a "dimension," constrained to give causally consistent operational experience enabling evolution to lower entropy states through free-will choice.
- He claims it may be possible to derive many general laws of PMR physics — the rule-set TBC applies to create space-time — by studying interactions between reality cells, expecting the space-time instruction-set to be a collection of general, broad, high-level fundamental laws, just as classical mechanics flows from a few simple Newton's laws and electricity/magnetism (macro level) is fully contained within Maxwell's four Equations.
- He claims at least some of the instruction-set implementing the space-time rule-set may be contained within the geometry (pattern and relationship) of the space-time construct — how AUM's binary reality cells form the structure of space-time and how their interaction is constrained — and that deriving physics from the structure of space-time is not as crazy as it sounds because an entity's basic structure often defines the nature of what can be built with or upon it.
- He claims Albert Einstein didn't think this idea was nutty either: Einstein spent the last half of his professional life trying to derive a unified field theory from the PMR side of the larger reality, never succeeded, but "his intuition was absolutely correct" — his PMR-only mind-set was simply too limited by the cultural beliefs of his day to contain the solution.
- He claims that what's being sought is a self-consistent logic at the reality-cell level of space-time that AUM-TBC may have used as part of a larger gedanken experiment to evolve portions of the space-time rule-set — not necessarily the actual approach, only one that promises some possibility of success (with a cross-reference that the concept of reality cells was developed in Chapter 26, Book 1).
- He claims he personally does not need to postulate reality-cell structures and rules and derive physics from them (which would require "a monster aside" of 300 technical pages), because Steven E. Kaufman, "a contemporary resident of PMR," has already derived the basics of PMR physics from the theoretical structure of a consciousness-derived, space-time, reality-cell-based instruction-set, in his book Unified Reality Theory: The Evolution of Existence Into Experience (Destiny Toad Press, 2002, ISBN 09706550-1-0), also readable online at http://www.unifiedreality.com.
- He claims Kaufman starts with simple binary reality cells in an Absolute Unbounded Oneness (AUO) — Kaufman does not use that term, but Campbell says the concept is identical — assumes basic things about how information transmits between adjacent reality cells, and derives the PMR physics of electromagnetism, gravity, force, and energy.
- He claims a little reverse engineering of Kaufman's unified reality theory yields many concepts of classical physics, relativity, and quantum mechanics, falling out entirely from the properties of reality cells that exist only in relation to themselves within an "AUO-digital-consciousness-energy-thing"; Kaufman derives basic physics from the relational cellular structure of a space-time construct within consciousness by analyzing a structural model postulated to exist within the mind-space of AUM and the calculation-space of TBC.
- He claims Kaufman "opened the door," showing one can make a logical analytical attempt to understand the instruction-set implementing the space-time rule-set, laying out a detailed process and mechanism explaining the phenomenology (rule-set) of PMR based on the causality of NPMRN, demonstrating how PMR might logically arise from the lowest level of cellular structure within AUM and TBC.
- He claims Einstein and the best physicists/mathematicians of his day tried to describe physics in terms of the geometry of space-time, while Kaufman took the next step of describing space-time physics in terms of the geometry and structure of NPMR — a process that, surprisingly, yields algorithms and processes leading to contemporary PMR physics.
- He allows the reader may not agree with all of Kaufman's specific assumptions or accept all his conclusions, but insists coherent and reasonable bridges have been constructed connecting AUO, consciousness reality cells, space-time reality cells, and twentieth/twenty-first-century physics; he predicts Kaufman's path will need to be widened and extended by others, and credits Kaufman with helping satisfy one of the most important requirements for any "wannabe Big TOE": that it fully contains the little TOE.
- He claims PMR physics is contained within the rule-set that defines and constrains the experience of individuated consciousnesses perceiving a virtual physical reality — PMR may be thought of as a digital simulation executing within a greater digital consciousness, and contemporary PMR physics simply represents a portion of the rule-set defining the PMR virtual reality.
- He claims the Big TOE cannot conflict with known little-TOE facts and must broaden overall understanding of little-TOE phenomena presently unexplainable — including psi effects, mind, consciousness, human purpose, and the efficacy of intuition — and asserts "My Big TOE fully meets that criteria."
- He claims Kaufman basing his work on an Absolute Unbounded Oneness (AUO) assumption is a very good sign, because Chapter 18 (Book 1) established that logic demands a successful Big TOE have at least one mystical or metaphysical leg to stand on; conversely, any TOE without a connection to what appears mystical from the PMR point of view must logically be only a little TOE, fundamentally incapable of dealing with our beginnings, our minds and consciousness, or us as whole beings — and that to view a whole and complete human entity, one must step out of the PMR box.

## Concepts

- **Big TOE** — the larger-reality theory that contains and gives context to the little TOE; a "wannabe Big TOE" must fully contain the little TOE and not conflict with its known facts while explaining what it cannot.
- **The belief wall** — the "invisible wall constructed of belief" scientists and philosophers hit at the outer boundary of their little picture, where it meets the larger reality; requires a conceptual breakthrough that fundamentally expands their reality to pass through.
- **The box metaphor** — "You cannot get out of the box while defining the box to be everything that exists"; belief that one's box is All That Is makes everything outside it look delusional, and makes little TOEs describing the box look like big TOEs to those trapped inside.
- **Reality cells / instruction-set / space-time rule-set (Chapter 31 recap)** — reality cells are analogous to transistors on a processor chip (binary, on/off); the processor's instruction-set is analogous to basic cognitive functioning within AUM; the space-time rule-set is analogous to assembly-language algorithms; our experience is generated by an AUM-TBC-to-individuated-consciousness interface analogous to an object-oriented C++ simulation in which we are the objects; AUM is the computer, programmer, and operating system.
- **DSP (Digital Signal Processor) chip analogy** — a new analog (not from Chapter 31) for space-time: understanding the rules governing energy transfer to/from transistors in a special-purpose microprocessor reveals its basic design/implementation/capacity relationships; likewise, understanding information-transfer rules between space-time reality cells should yield physics's most fundamental relationships.
- **Dimension** — defined here as a subset of digital calculation-space within which a specialized space-time virtual reality trainer operates, constrained by the space-time rule-set to provide causally consistent experience.
- **Fundamental/general laws** — the expected character of the space-time instruction-set: general, broad, high-level statements from which detailed implications (science) are derived, exemplified by Newton's laws (classical mechanics) and Maxwell's four Equations (electricity and magnetism at the macro level).
- **Gedanken experiment** — the kind of logical structure Campbell proposes AUM-TBC may have used to evolve portions of the space-time rule-set from a differentiated reality-cell mental construction of aware consciousness; explicitly offered as "not necessarily the approach, but only an approach that promises some possibility of success."
- **AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness)** — Campbell's term for the starting point Kaufman uses (simple binary reality cells within it) without using that name himself; Campbell says the concept is identical to his own AUO.
- **Unified Reality Theory** — Steven E. Kaufman's book (Unified Reality Theory: The Evolution of Existence Into Experience, Destiny Toad Press, 2002, ISBN 09706550-1-0; also at http://www.unifiedreality.com), which derives PMR physics of electromagnetism, gravity, force, and energy from simple binary reality cells and assumptions about information transmission between adjacent cells.
- **NPMRN** — the causality basis Campbell says Kaufman uses to explain the phenomenology (rule-set) of PMR in his detailed process/mechanism.
- **Unified field theory** — the goal Einstein pursued for the last half of his professional life from the PMR side alone; he never succeeded, though (per Campbell) his intuition was correct.
- **Mystical/metaphysical leg requirement (recalled from Chapter 18, Book 1)** — logic demands a successful Big TOE have at least one mystical or metaphysical leg to stand on; any TOE lacking a connection to what appears mystical from the PMR viewpoint must be only a little TOE, incapable of addressing our beginnings, minds/consciousness, or us as whole beings.
- **Psi effects, mind, consciousness, human purpose, efficacy of intuition** — the presently unexplainable little-TOE phenomena Campbell says a Big TOE must broaden understanding of; listed as the criteria My Big TOE meets.
