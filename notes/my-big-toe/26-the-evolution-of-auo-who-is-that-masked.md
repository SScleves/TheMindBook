# Ch 26 — The Evolution of AUO: Who Is That Masked Consciousness Thing, and What Can It Do?

## In one paragraph
Campbell picks up his origin story at the point where AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness) is, as far as our reality is concerned, the only thing that exists — no space, no time, only a dim awareness interacting with itself. He argues that thought, mind, consciousness or spirit are the only things we know that are not bounded by space and time, so AUO is primordial consciousness — the "fundamental non-material material of existence" — from which both our individual consciousness and the physical universe can be derived. Warning against anthropomorphizing AUO (it is the substance of consciousness, not the attribute of being conscious), Campbell describes how the Fundamental Process of evolution, rewarding entropy reduction in a self-organizing digital system, drives AUO to its first achievement: creating a duality within itself — a local disturbance relative to its uniformity. Repeating that disturbance yields regularly recurring events (the proto-clock that will later invent time), and multiplying such disturbances yields billions of "reality cells." By chapter's end AUO stands at the stage of a huge, structureless multi-celled behemoth — "no structure, no order, no specialization of function — yet" — poised to get organized.

## Core claims
- Campbell claims that at this stage of the model AUO is apparently the only thing that exists that is interactive with, or relevant to, our reality; anything hypothetically beyond AUO can be ignored or conceptualized as a non-interactive void.
- Campbell argues that thought, mind, consciousness and spirit are the only things known to us that exist outside the bounds of our space and time — therefore AUO is primordial consciousness, and both our individual consciousness and the physical universe can be derived from it. ("Absolutely!")
- Campbell asserts (as a preview of Sections 3–5) that consciousness is the basic media from which reality is formed, and that a more structured, ultra-low-entropy form of this primordial consciousness energy can evolve into the operative digital mind that animates and defines the content of our larger reality.
- Campbell warns against the anthropomorphic trap: AUO is not (and never becomes) conscious or sentient the way a human is; it is an energy form with the potential to evolve awareness — the primordial substance of consciousness, not the attribute of being conscious.
- Campbell defines consciousness as the result of evolution offering profitability to a self-organizing complex system of relational digital content; a system able to lower its entropy through self-organization will eventually host some level of consciousness.
- Campbell holds that AUO can be conceptualized as apparently infinite potential energy — the potential of a digital system to self-organize by reducing its average entropy through evolution; lower entropy equals higher usable energy.
- Campbell claims AUO "figured out" what to do with itself the same way biological cells did: extremely slowly, under the relentless pressure of evolution nudging all systems toward greater profitability, through huge numbers of more or less random internal experiments evaluated for profitability.
- Campbell's first evolutionary step for AUO is the creation of a duality within itself — "this vs. not this" — a local disturbance or distortion relative to its uniformity; distortion exists only in relation to uniformity.
- Campbell claims a single localized modification of the uniform state is all that is required to derive ourselves, our physics, and all the rest of our reality, physical and nonphysical.
- Campbell argues that a disturbance intentionally re-established and released over and over becomes a regularly recurring event that evolves into a precisely recurring event — which might eventually be used to invent time ("the first tick-tock").
- Campbell notes that evolutionary "discoveries" (his scare quotes) are not well-defined events but diffused happenings over long periods; profitability means improved survivability for bio-organisms and lower entropy for consciousness organisms.
- Campbell insists the mathematical details of how AUO changes state (sinusoids, sawtooths) are unimportant; the main message is that AUO, like earth's primordial biological cells, evolves to become dimly aware that it can affect changes in its state.
- Campbell claims AUO eventually discovers it can create two, three, hundreds, billions of simultaneous disturbances (reality cells), since an approximately infinite oneness should easily support quantity far beyond our comprehension — leaving it a huge multi-celled but very simple behemoth with no structure or specialization yet.
- Campbell holds that awareness, however dim, always creates ever more possibilities to explore, and complexity, however simple, always generates greater possibilities; growth in reality cells is self-feeding because each increment creates new possible configurations of the larger system.

## Concepts
- **AUO (Absolute Unbounded Oneness)** — Campbell's primordial consciousness: at this point apparently the only thing that exists (no space, no time), dimly aware and interacting primarily with itself. It is "the primordial substance of consciousness itself, not the attribute of being conscious," and can also be conceptualized as an apparently infinite potential energy.
- **primordial consciousness** — What AUO is or represents: an energy form that, like earth's early carbon-based bio-forms, has the potential to evolve awareness — not human-like sentience.
- **consciousness** — Campbell defines it here as "the result of evolution offering profitability to a self-organizing complex system of relational digital content"; the basic media from which reality is formed, "the fundamental non-material material of existence," and the source of the organizational energy from which all individuated existence is derived.
- **Fundamental Process (of evolution)** — Campbell's universal evolutionary driver: it optimizes the self-modifiable form, function, and potential energy that consciousness provides, drives change at all levels, and relentlessly nudges all systems toward greater profitability.
- **evolutionary profitability** — The criterion by which random internal experiments are evaluated: improved survivability for bio-organisms, lower entropy for consciousness organisms.
- **entropy (of consciousness)** — The quantity evolution drives down in AUO; lower average entropy means higher usable energy. A digital system's potential to reduce its average entropy through self-organization is its potential energy, and a system that can do this will eventually host consciousness.
- **potential energy (of digital consciousness)** — "The potential to self-organize, which is derived from the potential of a digital system to reduce its average entropy through evolution."
- **digital consciousness** — Campbell's forward-looking framing: over the coming chapters a digital model of highly aware consciousness will be derived from a single monolithic consciousness system driven to ever lower average entropy by evolutionary profitability.
- **duality / dualization** — AUO's first creative act: "This vs. not this" — altering its uniformity to create a local disturbance, so that distorted exists relative to non-distorted. Dualization is AUO's opportunity space for expressing differentiation of one part of itself relative to another.
- **disturbance / distortion / non-uniformity** — A localized modification of AUO's uniform state; it exists only in relation to uniformity. One such modification is sufficient to derive ourselves, our physics, and all the rest of reality.
- **unipolar oscillation** — A mode of dualizing that cycles uniform → positive-disturbance → uniform → positive-disturbance, e.g. in the form [sin(wt)] (Fig. 2-1), or perhaps [sin(wt)+1]/2, sin²(wt), or a linear sawtooth (Fig. 2-2).
- **bipolar oscillation** — Cycling uniform → positive-disturbance → uniform → negative-disturbance → uniform, e.g. sin(wt) (Fig. 2-3). "Positive" and "negative" are opposite disturbances such as good/bad, hot/cold, thought/no thought — Campbell deliberately excludes spatial opposites (here/there, up/down, in/out) because space is not a native construct of AUO and has not yet been discovered.
- **reality cell** — Campbell's coinage, named in honor of the biology analogy: the singular relative disturbance in AUO, analogous to the one-celled organisms of earth's primordial soup. AUO multiplies these into billions with, as yet, no structure, order, or specialization.
- **cell specialization** — The biological analogy for what comes next: something analogous to it eventually evolves within AUO to create internal dimensions that support various realities populated by individuated units of consciousness.
- **time (invention of)** — Not yet existent; a precisely recurring event might eventually be used to invent it — "the first tick-tock, tick-tock, tick-tock as it were."
- **space** — Explicitly not a native construct of AUO and not yet discovered by it at this stage.
- **anthropomorphic trap** — The error Campbell warns readers against: assuming AUO is or becomes conscious/sentient the way a human being is.
- **individuated consciousness** — What the Fundamental Process eventually defines through growth opportunities — "individuated consciousness content such as yourself," derived from consciousness's organizational energy.
- **Big TOE** — Campbell's Big Theory Of Everything, said to come slowly into focus over the next three sections, culminating (by end of Section 5) in understanding how physical reality is created, how it operates, and what purpose it serves.

## Experiments, examples & case studies
- **The ocean-and-ripple analogy (continued from earlier chapters)**: the calm, still ocean the reader floated in "a few chapters ago" to gain an intuitive sense of apparently infinite absolute unbounded oneness "now has evolved to the point of making a tiny ripple. With lots of practice and some good luck, ripples eventually become waves and whirlpools." Shows the first duality — non-uniformity relative to uniformity.
- **The biological-evolution analogy (primordial soup)**: one-celled critters "discovered" multi-celled configurations were more effective, then that cell specialization made a more efficient organism — with "discovered" used in the evolutionary sense of diffuse happenings over long stretches after huge numbers of random internal experiments evaluated for profitability. AUO evolves the same way, and the "reality cell" is named for this analogy. Campbell tracks the stages: single disturbance = one-celled organism in primordial soup; billions of disturbances = "huge multi-celled but still very simple behemoth"; "Here a cell, there a cell, everywhere a cell cell ... (reality cell, that is)." He notes we ourselves are composed of billions upon billions of cells.
- **The oscillation model with figures**: Figures 2-1, 2-2, and 2-3 show three examples of regularly occurring continuous change between non-uniform and uniform states — Fig. 2-1 unipolar [sin(wt)], Fig. 2-2 a linear unipolar sawtooth, Fig. 2-3 bipolar sin(wt). Other unipolar forms offered: [sin(wt)+1]/2 and sin²(wt). Campbell says the trigonometry is unimportant to the binary point (disturbed vs. not-disturbed); he uses it to show the range of possibilities "and because the logical side of Mother Nature seems to be partial to sinusoids."
- **The proto-clock thought experiment**: AUO discovers a disturbance, returns to uniform, intentionally re-establishes it, returns to uniform, and so on — a regularly recurring event that evolves into a precisely recurring one, which "might eventually be used to invent time (the first tick-tock, tick-tock, tick-tock as it were)." Campbell cautions AUO has a lot more evolving to do before anything as advanced as uniform vibration or oscillation.
- **Non-spatial opposite pairs**: to define bipolar disturbance without space, Campbell lists good/bad, hot/cold, more/less, sharp/dull, bright/dim, acid/base, hungry/full, thought/no thought — pointedly avoiding here/there, up/down, in/out because AUO has not discovered space.
- **The "glance forward" device**: Campbell deliberately previews where the argument leads (consciousness as the basic media of reality; an ultra-low-entropy digital mind animating our larger reality; full picture by end of Section 5) so readers can structure the concepts — while warning them not to jump to "wrong-headed" conclusions that might seem obvious now.

## Thread
This chapter is the second beat of Campbell's cosmogenesis-of-consciousness narrative in Section 2: having introduced AUO as absolute unbounded oneness (the "calm, still ocean" of a few chapters back) and the Fundamental Process of evolution as his two basic assumptions, he now sets evolution loose on AUO. The chapter's job is threefold: identify AUO as primordial consciousness (because only mind is known to exist outside space and time), fence off the anthropomorphic misreading, and derive the first structures — duality, oscillation, and reality cells — from nothing but a uniform medium plus evolutionary pressure toward lower entropy. It also plants the seeds of later machinery: the precisely recurring event that will invent time, and the cell-specialization analogy that will yield internal dimensions, individuated consciousness, and (per the explicit preview) the ultra-low-entropy digital mind of the larger reality, promised to be fully assembled by the end of Section 5. It ends on a cliffhanger — "AUO is about to get organized" — handing off directly to the next chapter.
