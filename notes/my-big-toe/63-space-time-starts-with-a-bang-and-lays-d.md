# Ch 63 — Space-Time Starts With a Bang and Lays Down the Rules

## In one paragraph
Campbell recapitulates and then extends his account of how our physical universe came to be: PMR is a simulated virtual reality computed within its own calculation space inside TBC (a portion of AUM's digital mind), and its space-time rule-set was designed to constrain and guide the interactions of individuated units of consciousness so their quality could evolve efficiently. He explains that the rule-set defines what perceptions and energy exchanges are allowed, while each free-will player uniquely interprets the raw data it receives — this rule-set-physics *is* what we experience as physical reality. He then narrates the creation of the universe itself: define a space-time rule-set (the complete physics of PMR), wind it up with a huge simulated potential energy, and let time begin to iterate — a Big Bang — after which TBC computes every ramification as the Fundamental Process expands the system into all possibilities and progresses the profitable ones. Every state at every time increment (DELTA-t) is saved in TBC. The chapter closes with Campbell's insistence that there is only consciousness — even rocks are consciousness constructs — and that we live simultaneously under nested rule-sets (like nested layers of law), needing to heed only the local ones (PMR physics) until we venture beyond our local reality.

## Core claims
- Campbell claims the space-time rule-set evolved to constrain the interactions and communications of individuated units of consciousness (sentient beings) inhabiting PMR, which is a simulated virtual reality computed within its own calculation space (dimension) within TBC — itself a portion of the digital mind of AUM.
- Campbell claims the design requirements of the lower-level space-time rule-set (which defines PMR's environment and physics) were developed to optimize the effective and efficient evolution of consciousness quality within NPMR-N: demanding personal responsibility as a condition of existence and providing strong immediate feedback to guide learning.
- Campbell claims there was a need for a "recyclable awareness" that resets itself periodically, maintaining a high growth rate by re-cycling through the PMR trainer whenever its evolutionary growth becomes unacceptably slow, thereby avoiding belief-trapping itself into a mental corner. Personal beliefs and fears are eliminated cycle to cycle, but cultural beliefs and fears pass from generation to generation — human culture and history record our cumulative progress.
- Campbell claims our space-time is one of many space-time rule-sets within TBC; it defines a dimension of experience by bounding what is allowed and possible in that simulation — including allowable energy exchange, message traffic, and thus the allowable perception-set for all indigenous space-time entities.
- Campbell claims each individual consciousness unit must interpret the raw sensor data it receives — using its abilities, memory, history, capacity, quality, and free will — to transform data into meaningful information and thereby develop more profitable intents leading to lower entropy.
- Campbell claims it is this rule-set-physics that drives and defines the experience each individuated consciousness interprets as physical reality; we exist only as nonphysical units of individuated consciousness but experience "physical" reality within the constraints of the rule-set.
- Campbell claims the universe was created by defining the rule-set (the complete set of PMR physics), winding it up with a huge simulated potential energy, and letting time iterate — the Big Bang is the potential-energy "reality spring" beginning to unwind within TBC as the simulation's time increment begins to iterate.
- Campbell claims the space-time rule-set defines causality while the Fundamental Process experimentally expands the evolving system into all possibilities, progressing the profitable ones; AUM merely stands back and watches TBC compute the ramifications.
- Campbell claims an experimenter could run this universe-creation simulation repeatedly, sampling over random components, rule-sets, and parameters, saving the best candidates to non-volatile memory for culturing — and speculates about how many false starts preceded our fully evolved rule-set.
- Campbell claims (recalling AI Guy) that virtual reality rule-sets occur at two distinct levels: higher-level rules defining goals and purpose, and lower-level rules defining local physics and causality. The higher-level rule-set nudges us in the right evolutionary direction through our intuition and conscience.
- Campbell claims TBC progresses space-time by sequentially incrementing time; every state that develops, beginning at t=0, is tracked, recorded, and saved at every DELTA-t increment.
- Campbell claims there is only consciousness — everything, including rocks and "dead doornails," is a consciousness construct; all experience results from applying rule-sets to our individuated consciousness, and when we are operationally aware in nonphysical NPMR-N realities, our experience is defined by those realities' rule-sets instead.
- Campbell claims we live under all applicable rule-sets simultaneously but practically need only be aware of the local rules (PMR physics) until we venture beyond our local PMR neighborhood.

## Concepts
- **space-time rule-set** — Campbell's term for the set of rules (including the complete physics of PMR) that defines the environment, causality, and boundaries of what is allowed and possible within the space-time simulation; it constrains energy exchange and message traffic between players, thereby defining the allowable perception-set for all indigenous entities.
- **PMR (physical-matter reality)** — our physical universe, which Campbell says is a simulated virtual reality computed within its own calculation space (dimension) inside TBC; a "consciousness evolution trainer" where we live, work, and play.
- **TBC (The Big Computer)** — a portion of the digital mind of AUM that computes and executes the space-time simulation, creates the logical causal structure of PMR, and tracks, records, and saves every state of the evolving reality at every time increment.
- **AUM** — the larger consciousness whose digital mind contains TBC; AUM is "the computer, the application, and the operating system," whose clock ticks 10^36 times faster than ours, and who stands back and watches TBC compute the results of the rule-set and the Fundamental Process.
- **NPMR / NPMR-N** — nonphysical-matter reality (and our particular subset N of it): the larger mind-space within which PMR exists as a subset and within which the evolution of consciousness quality is being optimized; NPMR-N realities have their own rule-sets that define experience there.
- **Fundamental Process** — Campbell's evolutionary logic: expanding a system into all possible states and continuing (progressing) those that are profitable; applied together with the space-time rule-set it drives the dynamic evolution of the simulated universe.
- **DELTA-t** — the discrete time increment by which TBC sequentially progresses the space-time simulation; every DELTA-t, all simulated elements and players move to new values and configurations, and every resulting state is saved (beginning with t=0).
- **individuated consciousness** — a discrete unit or "puddle" of AUM consciousness (a sentient being) with its own abilities, memory, history, capacity, quality, and free will; we exist only as such nonphysical units while experiencing physical reality through the rule-set.
- **recyclable awareness** — an awareness designed to reset itself periodically, recycling through the PMR virtual experience trainer whenever growth becomes unacceptably slow, so it can indefinitely accumulate the rapid early growth of a new entity while individually shedding personal beliefs and fears each cycle.
- **belief-trapping** — the process by which an evolving entity corners itself mentally with accumulated beliefs and fears, slowing its growth — the problem recycling is designed to avoid.
- **player** — any entity that can interact within the simulation: sentient (another being), non-sentient (a rock, a river, a sand storm), or energetic; players appear solid, take up space, and must cooperatively share limited space and resources.
- **free will** — the intent-directed agency of each actor within the constrained experience-space; individual actions and interactions follow each actor's free will intent, and each player freely interprets received messages.
- **entropy** — Campbell's measure of consciousness disorganization: profitable intents lead to lower entropy; more generally, as the material simulation unfolds entropy decreases in some places while increasing in others, and the growth of inanimate objects specifically implies higher organization and a decrease of entropy.
- **Big Bang** — in Campbell's model, the moment the wound-up "potential energy reality spring" begins to unwind within TBC as the time increment driving the simulation begins to iterate.
- **two-level rule-sets** — recalling AI Guy: higher-level rules define goals and purpose (working through intuition and conscience to urge our higher selves "to be the right thing"), while lower-level rules define local physics and causality.
- **consciousness evolution trainer / simulation trainer** — Campbell's characterization of PMR as a "huge multiplayer simulation trainer" providing education by total experiential immersion.
- **thought-form** — how new entities are birthed within NPMR-N: existing entities focus enough mental energy to produce ("materialize") a new thought-form; space-time entities, by comparison, reproduce by creating a new bounded form within the space-time manifold.
- **mind-space** — the mental (nonphysical) space of AUM within which the grand simulation runs; Campbell calls the simulation "the mother of all gedanken experiments" in mind-space.
- **space-time manifold** — the evolving space-time itself, which once launched "is on its own to evolve however it evolves according to the Fundamental Process," with AUM perhaps tinkering a little here and there.

## Experiments, examples & case studies
- **The universe-creation recipe (the chapter's central model)** — Campbell walks through how "someone or some digital thing might create an entire virtual universe such as ours": (1) start with a rule-set defining constrained space-time including the complete set of PMR physics; (2) wind it up with a huge simulated potential energy; (3) let it evolve within TBC per the Fundamental Process as the time increment begins to iterate — the Big Bang ("Big Bang — take one! Cameras! Action! Roll 'em!"). Simulated energy changes form into heat, mass, motion; new players are constantly generated (hot plasma, galaxies, solar systems, planets, primordial ooze, and critters); matter individuates and coagulates into "droplets called stars and planets." One eventual result of the apparent local stability may be a universe such as ours.
- **The multiplayer simulation/video-game analogy** — "Imagine a huge multiplayer simulation trainer"; Campbell likens watching the universe evolve to playing a video game called "Cosmological Evolution in Space-time," which he jokes would be "a best-seller within AUM's NPMR-N subset of mind-space." He adds that AUM won't need to "re-boot TBC in the middle of your favorite reality program" because AUM is the computer, the application, and the operating system.
- **Earthbound computer games invoking the solidity rule** — from the rule that two players cannot occupy the same space at the same time (which Campbell labels "Newton's first Law") comes the experienced solidity, spatial extent, and shared limited resources of 3D space-time; Campbell notes our own interactive computer games already invoke this same rule, so "it evidently is not difficult to do."
- **The experimenter re-running the simulation (petri-dish metaphor recalled)** — an experimenter could run the physical-universe creation simulation as often as it wished, sampling over random components, rule-sets, and parameters, saving the best to non-volatile memory for "maintaining and culturing" — explicitly recalling the earlier petri-dish metaphor. Campbell wonders how many false starts occurred before our rule-set was fully evolved and asks, "Do you think AUM has it right this time?"
- **The flat-earth scale analogy** — the churning universe appears stable in the little picture because it is animated on such a grand scale, just as the earth appears flat because it is so large relative to the local awareness of a walking man.
- **The nested-laws analogy** — the multiple rule-sets applying to our consciousness across NPMR's dimensions are like international, national, state, county, and city law, subdivision covenants, and "the law our mom lays down when we have been naughty": all apply all the time, but practically we attend only to local laws (PMR physics) until we travel beyond our local neighborhood.
- **AI Guy (recalled)** — Campbell refers back to his earlier AI Guy discussion to ground the claim that virtual-reality rule-sets occur at two levels: higher-level (goals and purpose) and lower-level (local physics and causality).
- **"Snug as bugs" picture of our situation** — after many DELTA-t increments, "here we are in our space-time PMR, snug as bugs in the fabric of a PMR space-time rug. Clueless about who we are, where we came from, or what we are supposed to do" — yet with an intuitive idea of what is going on because we are discrete chunks of AUM consciousness in a digital simulation-trainer.

## Thread
This chapter sits in Section 4 ("Solving the Mystery") as the payoff of the rule-set thread: having established AUM, TBC, the Fundamental Process, NPMR-N, and the design rationale for a space-time trainer in earlier chapters (it opens as an explicit "short recapitulation" and recalls the AI Guy discussion, the petri-dish metaphor, and the birthing of NPMR-N entities as thought-forms), it now runs the machinery forward: it shows how launching the rule-set with wound-up potential energy produces a Big Bang, cosmological evolution, and eventually us — clueless-but-snug individuated consciousnesses inside PMR. It cements the identification of physical reality with rule-set-constrained perception ("there is only consciousness") and installs the two-level rule-set picture (lower-level physics vs. higher-level purpose working through intuition and conscience), setting up subsequent chapters' elaboration of how PMR's mechanics, time, and probable realities operate within TBC.
