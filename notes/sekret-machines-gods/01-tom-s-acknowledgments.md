# Tom's Acknowledgments

## In one paragraph
Tom DeLonge thanks his wife Jennifer and children Ava and Jonas for supporting his interest in "all things weird." He credits Peter Levenda's research, speaking ability, words, and experience with helping him summarize the subject and gain the attention of the US Government, and thanks AJ Hartley for turning the project's esoteric material into a form ordinary people can understand. DeLonge closes by addressing unnamed government advisors, telling them that the project is already changing how people think about themselves and their world.

## Core claims
- DeLonge attributes his ability to summarize the project's subject and eventually gain "the ears of the US Government" in part to Peter Levenda's research, words, and experience.
- He says AJ Hartley made the esoteric knowledge vivid and accessible to ordinary people.
- DeLonge tells the unnamed government advisors that their work through the project has already begun to change the world by waking people up and changing their self-understanding and worldview.

## Concepts
- **Sekret Machines project** — The collaborative undertaking for which DeLonge credits Levenda, Hartley, and unnamed government advisors; in this acknowledgment it joins research, public explanation, creative presentation, and government access.
- **Esoteric knowledge** — The difficult or specialized material Hartley absorbed and, according to DeLonge, brought to life for ordinary people.
- **Government advisors** — Unnamed officials whom DeLonge treats as contributors to the project and credits with changing public thought through it.
- **Public understanding** — The project's intended effect of making people think differently about themselves and the world in which they live.

## Experiments, examples & case studies
- **Family support** — DeLonge thanks Jennifer, Ava, and Jonas for supporting his interests in "all things weird."
- **Peter Levenda's contribution** — DeLonge calls Levenda a brilliant researcher, eloquent spokesperson, and great friend. He says Levenda's words and experience enabled him to summarize the subject and eventually attract the US Government's attention.
- **AJ Hartley's contribution** — Hartley joined the project, absorbed its esoteric knowledge, and rendered it in a form DeLonge says ordinary people could understand.
- **Unnamed government advisors** — DeLonge does not identify them, but tells them their participation has already awakened people and altered how those people think about themselves and their world.

## Key quotes
- "support of my interests in all things weird" — DeLonge's reason for thanking his wife and children.
- "such a brilliant researcher" — his description of Peter Levenda.
- "get us the ears of the US Government" — the result DeLonge connects to Levenda's contribution.
- "bringing it all to life" — what he says AJ Hartley did with the esoteric knowledge.
- "People are waking up" — the change he attributes to the government advisors' work through the project.

## Thread
This acknowledgment presents the book as the product of family support, Levenda's research and articulation, Hartley's accessible storytelling, and information or guidance from unnamed government advisors. It also previews the project's ambition to alter public understanding rather than merely collect unusual material.

## Connections
- **Peter's Acknowledgments** — DeLonge's brief thanks to unnamed advisors is complemented by Levenda's fuller description of a roughly ten-person advisory group whose identities are mostly withheld.
- **The fiction and nonfiction project** — DeLonge's praise for Hartley explicitly connects esoteric research to its fictional presentation for ordinary readers.
